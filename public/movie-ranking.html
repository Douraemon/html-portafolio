The Best Movier Ever According To Douglas

My Top 3 movies Of All Time

The Godfather

It's an obvious option, but I'm never tired of watching this movie. I believe it is the "modern" Citizen Kane.

The Graduate

This movie hits me to my very core. I identified a lot with the main character. I feel certain attraction to old women, and I too was lost when I finished High School.

Amadeus

I watched this movie every year. The story is amazing, part fictional, part real. The acting of Mozart and Salieri is amazing. Lastly, the music is just perfect.
